## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Constants & Variables:** `let`, `make`
- ✅ **Full math expressions:** with operator precedence
- ✅ **BigInteger math:** no overflow, infinite size!
- ✅ **Simple commands:** `say`, `let`, `make`
- ✅ **Inline & multiline comments:** `//`, `/* ... */`
- ✅ **Script files** & **REPL** support

---
